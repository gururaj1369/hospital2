Values



  1. WE ACT WITH INTEGRITY

We treat everyone with respect and dignity. We’re thoughtful and considerate. We have a strong ethical conscience, so our first priority is to do right by our patients and those who entrust their care to us.

  1. WE OFFER AN EXCEPTIONAL STANDARD OF CARE

We are committed to providing equitable access to advanced scientific tools and techniques. We offer best scientific practice as standard – not just to those who can afford to pay extra.

  1. WE COMPLETE THE CYCLE

We pay close attention at every step of the treatment cycle, and make careful decisions about how our patients’ life-giving assets are cared for and treated in the laboratory setting. Our systems and processes mean we are well-placed to identify why a cycle was or was not successful, and to use this knowledge to inform future care.

  1. WE PROVIDE A SOURCE OF HOPE

We are committed to enabling our patients’ success throughout their journey with us. We do not give false hope, but we are dedicated to providing our patients with options that provide the best chance of conception for them.

  1. WE ALLOW WHAT’S UNIQUE ABOUT OUR PATIENTS TO GUIDE US

Everyone is different and our fertility care reflects this. What is right for one person or couple may not be for another. We take the time to truly understand our patients’ circumstances from every angle – physiological, lifestyle, social and emotional – and to offer care and options tailored to them.
